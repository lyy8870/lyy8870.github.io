Privacy Policy for CalorieTrack

Effective Date: March 1, 2025

CalorieTrack (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our mobile application, CalorieTrack (the “App”).

1. Information We Collect

Camera Access: Used only to take food photos for calorie estimation. Images are not stored or shared by us.

AI Processing: Photos are securely sent to third-party AI services (e.g., Hugging Face API) for food recognition only.

User Data: Height, weight, or goals you provide are stored locally or securely to personalize your calorie plan.

2. How We Use Information

  • Estimate calorie and nutrition info from images
  • Display personalized insights and goals
  • Improve app experience

3. Data Security

All data transfers use HTTPS. We apply reasonable safeguards to protect your information.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Limited image data may be transmitted temporarily to third-party AI providers for recognition purposes only.

5. Your Rights

You may delete your data or revoke camera permissions at any time via device settings.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13.

7. Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. The “Effective Date” will reflect the latest version.
